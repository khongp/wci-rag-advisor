About White RAG Investor

White RAG Investor is a free, AI-powered financial advisor built for physicians and high-income professionals.

The name is a double play on words:

  • RAG = Retrieval-Augmented Generation, the AI architecture powering the chatbot.
  • Rags = because let's be honest, those student loans hit different.

How It Works

Every answer is grounded in over a decade of physician-specific financial wisdom from the White Coat Investor blog.

Why It Was Built

Medical school teaches you how to save lives, not how to manage a six-figure debt load. This bot distills WCI knowledge into a conversation you can have at 2 AM after a 14-hour shift.
